Privacy Policy for Hanuman Chalisa Pro

Effective Date: May 10, 2026

Hanuman Chalisa Pro respects your privacy. This app is designed to provide devotional content, audio playback, prayer reading, and spiritual reminders in a safe and simple experience.

Information We Collect

Hanuman Chalisa Pro does not require account registration or user login. We do not directly collect personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location
  • Contacts
  • Photos or files

Advertising

This app may use Google AdMob to display family-safe advertisements. AdMob may collect limited device-related information such as:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information
  • App interaction data
  • Diagnostics and crash logs

Ads shown in the app are intended to follow Google Play Families policies and child-safe advertising guidelines.

Audio Playback

The app includes offline devotional audio playback features. Audio files are stored locally within the app and are not uploaded or shared externally.

Notifications and Reminders

Hanuman Chalisa Pro may use local notifications for devotional reminders and prayer alerts. These reminders are created locally on your device and are not connected to external tracking systems.

Children's Privacy

This app is designed to be family-friendly and suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect app data and maintain secure communication where required.

Third-Party Services

This app may use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase services (if enabled)

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
